About Me

My name is Inusah Adelga, an Information and Communication Technology education student with a growing focus on technology-driven learning and digital empowerment.

I am currently pursuing a Bachelor of Education in Information and Communication Technology (B.Ed ICT) at the University of Cape Coast, Ghana. My academic journey is shaped by a strong interest in how technology can be used to improve access to quality education, especially in underserved communities.

I am deeply passionate about IT-STEAM education and practical skill development. Beyond formal coursework, I actively learn and build with modern technologies to strengthen my problem-solving and development skills.

My current learning focus includes Python programming, JavaScript, and modern web technologies such as HTML and CSS. I am particularly interested in using these tools to create simple, effective digital solutions for education and community development.

In the long term, my vision is to design and support initiatives that expand digital literacy and technical skills among young people, helping them gain confidence, opportunity, and relevance in an increasingly digital world.
